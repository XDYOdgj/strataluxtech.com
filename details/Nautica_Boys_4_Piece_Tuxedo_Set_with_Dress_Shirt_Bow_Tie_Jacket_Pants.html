Nautica Boys' 4-Piece Tuxedo Set

Make your child the star of any formal event with this elegantly tailored Nautica Boys' 4-Piece Tuxedo Set. Designed for comfort without sacrificing style, this set features a classic single-breasted jacket, a crisp dress shirt with button-down collar, a stylish bow tie, and well-fitted suit pants. Every piece is crafted to ensure a polished look that lasts from morning to night with minimal effort. The incorporation of breathable fabrics and a fully lined jacket makes it ideal for all-day wear, while the satin tie and shawl lapel add a touch of sophistication and refinement.

Key Features

  • Elegant one-button single breasted design

Whether it's a school function, a family gathering, or a special celebration, this tuxedo set is the perfect choice for young boys.
