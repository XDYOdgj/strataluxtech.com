Visesunny Boys Surfacing Board Shorts

Where style meets functionality for the active little boy. These quick-dry, breathable board shorts are perfect for all-day adventures, whether at the beach, pool, or playground.

Designed for Adventure

These board shorts are built for boys aged 2-14 who love to explore. The elastic waistband ensures a secure and comfortable fit, while the slightly longer cut offers just the right amount of coverage.
