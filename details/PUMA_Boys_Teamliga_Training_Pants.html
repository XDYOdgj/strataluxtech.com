PUMA Boys' Teamliga Training 3/4 Pants

Elevate your training game with these stylish and functional 3/4 training pants from PUMA. Engineered for active lifestyles, they blend comfort, performance, and sustainability into one sleek package.

Featuring PUMA's advanced dryCELL technology, these pants help keep you cool and dry during intense workouts or casual wear. The moisture-wicking properties ensure that you stay light and ready for any challenge, whether it's hitting the gym, the trails, or a weekend game.

The 3/4-length cut offers a perfect balance between coverage and mobility, while the elastic waist provides a comfortable, secure fit for all-day wear. Crafted from a recycled polyester blend, these pants are not only eco-friendly but also durable and stylish.

With a sporty aesthetic and tailored fit, the Teamliga training pants are ideal for athletes who want to look great while performing at their best. The PEAK Perform design enhances functionality without compromising on fashion, making these pants versatile for every activity.

Key Features
  • Moisture-wicking dryCELL technology: Keeps you dry and comfortable in any condition.
  • Recycled polyester blend: Sustainable choice with eco-friendly performance.
  • 3/4-length cut: Offers freedom of movement and a modern silhouette.
  • Elasticated waist: Provides a snug and flexible fit for all-day wear.
  • PEAK Perform design: Combines functionality with stylish tailoring for a sporty look.
Why Choose These Pants?

This versatile pair is perfect for both everyday use and active pursuits like training, sports, or outdoor adventures. Whether you're wearing them for a quick jog or a casual day out, the comfort and style never compromise. These pants are designed to keep up with your energy, ensuring you're always supported, stylish, and ready to move.
